Introduction:

Kazakh-Russian Medical University is one of the largest private universities in the country (state license AB 0026246), has passed the state certification five times (1998, 2003, 2007, 2012, 2017 G.) and issues a state-issued diploma. Our history began in 1992, in the wake of major political and economic transformations, where an important place was given to the reform of science and higher education. It was at that time that Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Kazakhstan, President of the Academy of Medical Sciences of the Republic of Kazakhstan, member of the International Association of Surgeons, People’s Hero of Kazakhstan – Mukhtar Aliyevich Aliyev, together with Professor K.M. Maskeev and associate professors V.S. Golubchenko and O.A. Panova founded one of the first private universities in the CIS. Then it became known as the Kazakhstan Medical Institute (KMI). Later, in 2010, the university was renamed the Kazakh-Russian Medical University

The educational program of the university fully meets the state standard of education. At the same time, the University has successfully tested new forms and methods of study aimed at improving higher medical education in the Republic.

More than 200 teachers, including world-renowned doctors and major Kazakh and foreign scientists, are involved in the training process.

Students also have the opportunity to study and practice abroad in academic mobility programs.

Education is provided both on a contractual basis and under the State Education Grant.

We do not form large academic groups – only 6-10 people. This allows to organize individual approach to students.

Students are free to choose their language of instruction (Kazakh or Russian), and from the first year they study foreign languages intensively.
